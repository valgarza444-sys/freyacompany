# How to Update Your Website
### A simple guide for Freya & Company

Your website is **freyacompany.xyz**. You can change almost anything on it yourself —
text, photos, services, even the fonts — using your own admin panel. No coding, no
designer needed. This guide walks you through it. 💛

---

## 1. Logging in

1. Go to **https://freyacompany.xyz/admin/**
2. Enter your **email and password** (the one you set when you accepted your invite).
3. You're in! You'll see your content organized into sections on the left.

> **Forgot your password?** On the login screen click *"Forgot password?"* and follow the
> email link to reset it.

---

## 2. The golden rule ⭐

After you make any change, click the **"Publish"** button (top of the screen).
Your website updates automatically in **about 1 minute**. If you don't click Publish,
your change won't go live.

You'll usually go: **make your edit → Publish → wait a minute → refresh your website.**

---

## 3. Changing text (the words on your site)

**Homepage words:**
1. Click **"Business Info & Settings" → "Home Page."**
2. Edit any of the boxes — your hero intro, the welcome paragraph, the feature quote, etc.
3. Click **Publish.**

**Your business info (phone, email, Instagram, tagline):**
1. Click **"Business Info & Settings" → "Business Info."**
2. Update the field you want.
3. Click **Publish.**

**Your About story:**
1. Click **"Pages" → "About Page."**
2. Edit the intro line and the main story text.
3. Click **Publish.**

---

## 4. Changing photos 📸

**The big homepage banner photo:**
1. Click **"Business Info & Settings" → "Home Page."**
2. Click **"Hero Background Image"**, then **"Choose an image"** → upload your photo.
3. Click **Publish.**

**Your About photo:** *(Pages → About Page → Photo → upload → Publish)*

> **Photo tips:**
> - Use **large, high-quality** photos (landscape/wide works best for the banner).
> - Horizontal photos suit the banner; upright (portrait) photos suit the portfolio.
> - JPG or PNG are perfect.

---

## 5. Adding a past event to your Portfolio

This is probably what you'll do most often after a beautiful event!

1. Click **"Portfolio"** on the left → **"New Event."**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** — e.g. *"Blush Garden Bridal Shower"*
   - **Category** — e.g. *Wedding, Bridal Shower, Birthday*
   - **Date / Year** — e.g. *2026*
   - **Cover Image** — the main photo people see first (upload it)
   - **Gallery Images** — click *"Add"* to include more photos from the event
   - **Feature on homepage** — turn this on to show it on your homepage
   - **Description** — a sentence or two about the event (optional)
3. Click **Publish.**

To edit or remove an event later, just click it in the Portfolio list.

---

## 6. Editing your Services

1. Click **"Services"** on the left.
2. Click a service (Tablescaping, Backdrop Rentals, etc.) to edit its text, or **"New
   Service"** to add one.
3. Click **Publish.**

---

## 7. Changing your fonts (optional, fun!)

1. Click **"Business Info & Settings" → "Fonts & Theme."**
2. Pick fonts from the dropdowns (headings, body text, script accents, hero tagline).
3. Click **Publish**, wait a minute, and refresh your site to see the new look.

Every option is hand-picked to match your elegant, romantic style — you can't break it.

---

## 8. Where do quote requests go?

When someone fills out the **"Request a Quote"** form on your site, it's automatically
**emailed to you**. The email includes their name, contact info, event details, budget,
and any inspiration photo they attached.

> To reply, open the email and use the customer's email address shown in it to write back.

---

## A few reassurances 💛

- **You can't break anything.** Every change is saved separately, and your website builder
  keeps a history — mistakes can always be undone.
- **Take your time.** Nothing goes live until you click **Publish**.
- **Not sure about something?** Reach out to whoever set up your site — happy to help.

That's it! You now have full control of your website. Have fun with it. ✨
